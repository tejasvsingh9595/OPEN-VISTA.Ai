# OpenVistaAI
**Failure-Aware Career & Learning Navigator**

OpenVista AI is an AI-powered platform that helps learners avoid common failure points in learning and validate career choices early through real-world micro-internships.

Instead of only showing *what to learn*, PathPilot AI shows **where learners fail**, **why they fail**, and provides **simplified survival resources**. It also allows users to experience real job tasks in 15 minutes before committing months of effort.

---

## 📌 Problem Statement

Learners often drop out because:

* Roadmaps do not highlight difficult or high-failure areas
* Users choose careers without understanding daily job realities
* Lack of guidance leads to wasted time, money, and motivation

---

## 💡 Solution

OpenVista AI introduces two core innovations:

### 1️⃣ Anti-Roadmap (Churn Zones)

* Highlights steps where most learners struggle
* Explains why people fail
* Provides simplified explanations and quick resources

### 2️⃣ Micro-Internship Simulator (15-Minute Shift)

* Generates short real-world tasks based on job roles
* Allows users to experience actual work, not quizzes
* Provides AI feedback and fit score
